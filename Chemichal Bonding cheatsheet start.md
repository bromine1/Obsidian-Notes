### SR Exceptions
```ad-info
title: Exception

Alkali metals, when mixing with water, create a hydroxide(OH), such as the following:

$$
\begin{array}

 \text{Li} + H_2O \rightarrow \\
 Li+H_2O \rightarrow LiOH + H_2 \\
2 Li+2H_2O \rightarrow 2LiOH + H_2\\

\end{array}
```
